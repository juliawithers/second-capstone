Database tables: supplier table, customer table, inventory table, orders table(shows all past orders), sku table,

  • Supplier Table
  • Customer Table
  • Current Inventory Table
  • Past Order Table
  • SKU Table

Supplier table: id, supplier number, supplier company name, SKU, supplier contact name, supplier contact number, supplier contact email

Customer table: id, customer number, customer company name, customer contact name, customer contact number, customer contact email, customer contact billing address, customer contact ship-to address

Inventory table: id, SKU, QTY, Location in inventory

Orders table: history of all past orders. id, customer, SKU, QTY, PO number
